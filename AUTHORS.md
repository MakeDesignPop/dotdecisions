\# Decision Authors

Entries in `.decisions` may include the person who recorded or made the decision.

This is optional and intended for team environments.  

---

Format:

\[username] decision note

\## Example

```
2026-03-12
\[RK] Removed onboarding flow from v1.  
Adds weeks of work, not needed to validate the idea.

2026-03-13
\[SK] Updated hero layout.  
Improves CTA visibility.
```
